Cottesloe Residents & Ratepayers Association
Our aim is to be a forum for open discussion and a conduit of "all" opinions and information across the local community, between stakeholders and our Council, being both informative, critical, analytical and positive on issues and opportunities that are local to Cottesloe. We welcome views and input from our membership and the community. If you would like to join the Association, please click here for an application form. Our Association Constitution can be viewed by clicking here.
